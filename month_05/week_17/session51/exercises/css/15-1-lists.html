Impossible quiche recipe

  • Ingredients
    • 125g ham, chopped
    • 1 small onion, finenl chopped
    • 1 1/2 cups (180g) grated tawsty cheese
    • 1/3 cup (50g) self-raising flour
    • Salt & freshl ground pepper, to season
    • 4 eggs
    • 1 1/2cups(375ml) milk
  • Steps
    • Preheat oven to 200C. Grease a 5 cup capacity pie dish.
    • Combine ham, onion, cheese, flour, salt and pepper in a medium bowl.Scatter over base of dish.
    • Whisk eggs and milk together in a large jug and pour over ham mixture.Cook for about 40minutes or until puffed and golden.Cool slightly.Serve warm or cold.
